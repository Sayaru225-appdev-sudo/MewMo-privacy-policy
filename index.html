Privacy Policy for MewMo

Effective Date: May 9, 2026
Last updated: May 9, 2026

Thank you for using "MewMo" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This App is a simple memo tool. Its core design principle is: All your memos are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or upload any personal content you enter.

1. How We Handle Your Information (Core Principle)

MewMo itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information you enter. All memos remain only on your device. When you delete a memo (or mark it as finished), it may be recreated locally on a future date as designed by the App. No data is ever sent to us or any external server.

2. Third-Party Advertising Services (Google AdMob Banner Ads)

To support the free operation of this App, we use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements within the App. These banner ads help keep MewMo free for all users.

AdMob may automatically collect and process certain device information for the purpose of serving relevant banner ads, preventing fraud, and improving ad performance. This may include:

  • IP address (which may indicate approximate location)
  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID / AAID)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, viewability)
  • App usage signals related to ad rendering
  • Crash logs and technical diagnostics

This data is handled solely by Google under its own privacy policy. We do not have access to nor store any of this information ourselves. For complete details, please review Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can also learn about how to reset or limit ad tracking in your device's settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

3. Data Storage

Your memos are stored locally on your device. The App does not have any server to upload or sync your data. Local preferences (if any) are also stored only on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

MewMo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Regarding advertising, Google AdMob has its own policies concerning children and COPPA compliance. Banner ads served via AdMob may be configured to be non-personalized where required.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be revised accordingly. For significant changes, we will provide notice in the App's update notes. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes.
